What is AI workflow automation?

AI workflow automation is the integration of large language models (LLMs) and artificial intelligence agents directly into existing business processes and CRM systems. Unlike traditional automation which relies on rigid, rule-based triggers, AI-powered workflows can perform qualitative tasks like unstructured data classification, lead enrichment, activity summarisation, and decision-based routing, executing these complex operational tasks natively where your revenue teams already work every day.

Where AI Fits in RevOps

AI in RevOps goes beyond simple triggers. I implement LLMs directly inside your systems to handle lead enrichment, high-accuracy classification of companies, and real-time deal health assessments. By automating the summarisation of sales activity and applying routing logic that requires qualitative judgement rather than just rigid rules, you can eliminate operational bottlenecks and focus your team on high-leverage work.
