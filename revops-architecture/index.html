HubSpot RevOps Architecture & CRM Design

Pipeline structure, reporting, and data model design that makes HubSpot actually report the truth.

The problem with most CRM setups is they don't reflect the real sales motion, resulting in broken attribution and reports that nobody in leadership trusts. When object relationships are poorly mapped and lifecycle syncing is broken, manual workarounds multiply and data integrity deteriorates. I design and build clean HubSpot database architectures that map directly to your actual business operations, ensuring every stage, property, and calculation is clean and correct.

CRM & Data Model Design

We map and build custom object relationships, property architectures, and association schemas that reflect how deals actually move. By designing a clean data schema from the start, we eliminate duplicate entries, manual workarounds, and broken data relationships. The outcome is a scalable HubSpot foundation that handles complex transactional models without breaking.

Pipeline & Lifecycle Architecture

We define deal and pipeline stages with objective exit criteria so your sales velocity and forecasting metrics mean something. Lifecycle stages are programmatically synced across objects so that marketing and sales handoff logic doesn't leak. This structured flow ensures complete visibility and consistency across the entire customer lifecycle.

Reporting & Attribution

We design and build custom dashboards that answer real business questions and show your actual pipeline health. Attribution models are aligned to how your revenue happened, not just basic default rules. You get clear, trusted reports that prove marketing ROI and guide strategic revenue decisions.

Workflow & Automation

We engineer HubSpot workflow architectures that run smoothly and silently in the background without needing constant babysitting. By incorporating custom coded actions, we automate complex calculations, assignments, and API integrations with strict execution guardrails. This eliminates database errors, saves time, and keeps your operational data pristine.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a RevOps audit involve?

An audit involves a thorough assessment of your current CRM data model, pipeline structure, workflow architecture, and reporting gaps. The findings are delivered as a documented findings and recommendations report outlining exactly where issues lie and how to fix them.

How long does a RevOps engagement take?

The timeline depends entirely on the scope. A focused audit typically takes 1 to 2 weeks, while a full CRM rebuild and custom implementation takes 4 to 8 weeks.
